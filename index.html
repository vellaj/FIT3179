Chipotle Stores in the United States

Chipotle Store Finder

This map shows all Chipotle Stores as of August 2020. There are notably more stores along the East Coast than the West, and very few in the Central United States. Not surprisingly, this aligns with where most of the American population lives! Can you find a store near you?

Chipotle Stores and Population

This graph shows an exponentially positive relationship between the number of Chipotle Stores in a state and the state's population. That is, the more people there are in a state, the more Chipotle's there are! Median age doesn't seem to have much correlation with Chipotle store locations.

Store Breakdown by County

This visualisation shows a breakdown of stores within each state. It shows how many stores can be found within each county.

You can use the box underneath the bar chart to select a county! Notice how some counties have much more spread out stores than others.
